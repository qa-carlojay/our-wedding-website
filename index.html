Our Story

From band rehearsals to forever—music brought us together, and love kept the tempo.

By Reginwell

How We Met

During our college days, fate brought us together through music. I was in my second year, and CJ was in his first when we both joined an organization called Band Republic — a community of passionate musicians.

At the time, we were preparing to compete in a Battle of the Bands and urgently needed a bassist. CJ stepped in to fill the role. But somewhere between rehearsals and harmonies, the extrovert and the introvert found their rhythm.

"Who knew that audition would lead us to forever?"

As time went by, rehearsals turned into conversations. I was loud; He was quiet. I loved the spotlight; He preferred the background. Somewhere between band practices and shared moments, we grew closer without even realizing it.

If I'm being honest… I think I liked him first. But I wasn't so sure he liked me back. I told myself: "I will NOT message him for the whole day. If he doesn't message me, I'll stop waiting."

I waited. And waited. He didn't message me... not until exactly before midnight. His message? "Sorry for not messaging you the whole day. I was busy with my homework."

After months of getting to know each other, we finally made it official on January 4, 2017.
